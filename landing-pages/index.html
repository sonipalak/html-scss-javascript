Frequently asked questions

All carers identity are digitally checked including their right to work in the UK. All carers on the platform are insured as part of their Service Agreement

Please discuss with your carer and they can amend unpaid invoices. If the invoice has been paid please contact accounts@curamcare.com for any refund due

On approval of a Service Agreement you will be taken to the invoice. To make a payment you either enter your card details or selecting the non-recurring Direct Debit option.

Payment is help securely until the care is successfully delivered.

If a refund is required, please open the invoice and click the "contact support" button, enter your message and submit. This will hold the payment and a refund can be arranged.

Please contact the carer immediately, if no response then please contact us on info@curamcare.com

Simply hit the ‘Get Started’ button and complete the sign-up information. It is free to join!
